about me

Hi, my name is Sasri Dedigama. As a senior at the University of California, San Diego, I am majoring in Cognitive Science with a specialization in Human-Computer Interaction.

I love working with people from diverse backgrounds because it helps me expand my knowledge base, including learning skills I might not have been exposed to otherwise. I have a passion for using Human-Centered Design to create products and services for less fortunate communities. In my free time, I enjoy producing music, designing clothes and exploring nature through hiking, kayaking, and surfing.

If I had to break down what I do into 3 words, they would be: EMPATHIZE, CREATE, ITERATE.
