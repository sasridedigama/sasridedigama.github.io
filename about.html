About me

Hi, my name is Sasri Dedigama. I am a senior at the University of California San Diego majoring in Cognitive Science with a specialization in Human Computer Interaction.

I love working with people from diverse backgrounds as it helps me learn new skills and broaden my horizons. I have a passion for using Human Centered Design to create products and services for less fortunate communities. In my free time I enjoy producing music, designing clothes and exploring nature through hiking, kayaking and surfing.
