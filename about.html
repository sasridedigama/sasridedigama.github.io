about me

Hi, my name is Sasri Dedigama. I am a recent graduate from the University of California, San Diego, I majored in Cognitive Science with a specialization in Human-Computer Interaction.

I love working with people from diverse backgrounds because it helps me expand my knowledge base, including learning skills I might not have been exposed to otherwise. I have a passion for using Human-Centered Design to create products and services for less fortunate communities.

In my free time, I enjoy producing music, designing clothes and exploring nature through hiking, kayaking, and surfing.
